Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details. Once registered, you can log in and start enjoying the music.

How do I upload my music?

To upload your music, go to the "Upload" section from the navigation menu. Select your music file, add relevant details such as artist and song title, and click "Upload".

What are the benefits of a premium account?

With a premium account, you get access to exclusive content, ad-free listening, and higher quality audio. Stay tuned for more exciting features in the premium packages!

How do I contact support?

If you need assistance, you can contact our support team via the "Contact Us" page. We are here to help you with any issues you may encounter.
